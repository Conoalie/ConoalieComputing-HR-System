System Overview

The Conoalie Computing Human Resources Information System (HRIS) is an internal web-based platform designed to provide employees and managers with secure, consistent access to HR-related information.

The system supports the communication of company policies, employee benefits, training opportunities, and HR contact processes, ensuring that information is accessible across the organisation.
